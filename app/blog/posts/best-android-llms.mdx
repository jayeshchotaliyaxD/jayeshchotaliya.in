### Quick Troubleshooting

* **"Killed" Error:** Your phone ran out of RAM. Try a smaller model from Tier 1.
* **Slow Speed:** This is normal on old CPUs. Be patient!
* **Heat:** Your phone will get hot. That means it's working.
